Corridor research

The Claude research agent builds a dossier per city: does the city have an MLK street, its condition, the right officials at city hall / planning / economic development, and drafted outreach. Dossiers are committed to data/corridor-research/ and appear here. Outreach is always human-reviewed before anything sends.

Run it
Actions → "MLK corridor research" → Run workflow → enter the city, state, and organization from the application.
Requires
Repo secret ANTHROPIC_API_KEY (Settings → Secrets and variables → Actions).
